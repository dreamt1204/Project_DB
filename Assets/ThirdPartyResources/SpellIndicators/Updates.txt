________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Splats can now have their sizes adjusted dynamically. 

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tfi3Kcb-qtw

You can have a variety of spells use the same splat with scaling based on range for instance.

Please refer to the demo for default origin and offset values for accurate scaling.
